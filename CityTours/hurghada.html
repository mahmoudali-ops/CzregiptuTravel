A Mini Park Egypt is an open space that displays miniature buildings and models.

Mini Egypt Park offers a totally different experience compared to a traditional museum. This is a fantastic trip for our foreign guests which works as a live catalog for them to recognize the touristic attractions in Egypt, making it easier to choose where to go. Also, it is a fabulous outing for parents or grandparents with children and an extended learning environment for schools. This is not a hobby for children but is in fact an adult hobby. At the Museum, we would like to see children accompanied by adults, rather than children coming alone. It is the new generation of college-going adults who should cultivate this hobby, which will be immensely useful to them in real life. The Miniature Museum is very charming, and we should not underestimate its artistic quality. The (new) Miniature Museum includes a collection of 60 works of art by well-selected artists. These tiny volumes have not been bought; they are the results of years and years of gift-giving from ancestors. Behind every piece of art is a story of how it came to be part of Mini Egypt’s collection that is now housed in one place at Mini Egypt Park. At Mini Egypt Park, you will find our guide accompanying you on a rich tour of this great country in a few hours, as you can walk in from Cairo to Luxor in a few steps! Visit Abu Simbel temple and High dam at Aswan, passing Edfu temple to the legendary Luxor for the famous Karnak temple, heading towards Giza for the miraculous Pyramids, and talk to the Sphinx.

Hurghada Private City Tour

Would you like to get to know the real, original Hurghada? Would you like to visit the tourist attractions of the city? Are you tired of mass tourism and coffee trips? Would you like to see, be amazed and really enjoy?

We have put together the right mix for you. Of course you also have the possibility to design your tour with us individually! Hurghada city tour with Memnon Reisen is away from mass tourism!

Program of Hurghada Private City Tour

  • Mosque El Mina Masjid
  • Coptic Cathedral of Saint Shenouda
  • The fish market
  • The vegetable market
  • Hurghada Marina Boulevard

Description for Hurghada Private City Tour

AT 14:00, we will pick you up from your hotel in a quality air-conditioned vehicle.

The first stop takes you straight to the highest point in the city. From here a beautiful panoramic view of the entire city of Hurghada and the Red Sea opens up.

Then we go to the city pier. Let yourself be enchanted by the beautiful yachts and the harbor atmosphere as you sail along a leisurely stroll. We continue our tour to the fish market. Next stop is the oldest church in the city, the Coptic Church and the largest seaside mosque.

From there we will drive to the vegetable and fruit market in the old town. We then drive to a local cafe to enjoy mint tea or Egyptian coffee.

If you have a special request, you can discuss it with our guide. We will stop at all interesting places.

Around 19:00, we will take you back to your hotel.

Our tour of Hurghada will be absolutely thrilling and you will get many new experiences from Hurghada.
