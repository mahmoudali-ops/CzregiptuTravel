Program of Private Luxor Day Tour

  • Karnak Temple
  • Colossi of Memnon
  • Mortuary Temple of Hatshepsut
  • Valley of the Kings
  • Lunch (Egyptian specialties)
  • Nile excursion lasts 1 hour (FREE only with us)

Description for Private Luxor Day Tour

You will be picked up early in the morning. And then you drive on in a modern, comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle through Safaga on a newly built desert road of approx. 225 km to the Nile Valley in Qena.

Along the way, you take a short break to go to the bathroom or stretch your legs.

Then drive along a densely populated country road to Luxor. (About 60 km).

Upon arrival in Luxor, enjoy your excursion to Luxor. First of all, you will visit the largest temple complex in the world with a huge pillared hall. The bus will take you to Fiben West, where you will have lunch in a typical Egyptian cafe.

After your lunch break, explore the world famous Valley of the Kings with an inside guided tour of the royal tombs. New!!!! Taking photographs of graves with a mobile phone without a flash is now allowed free of charge. Then, head to the terrace of Queen Hatshepsut Temple, a fun spot for photographers to take great pictures. Your visit ends at the impressive Colossi of Memnon.

Then you head back to Hurghada. It’s time to look back at the impressions of the day and perhaps observe life on the Nile. You will be returned to your hotel at around 20:00. One day tour to Luxor and the Valley of the Kings will be remembered for a long time.

Program of Luxor Small Group Tour

Highlights of the Tour

  • Valley of the Kings
  • Temple of Queen Hatshepsut
  • Colossi of Memnon
  • Lunch at a local restaurant
  • Karnak Temple
  • Optional Nile boat trip

Description for Luxor Small Group Tour

We pick you up early in the morning from your hotel in Hurghada by air-conditioned mini-bus.

During the drive to Luxor, you will enjoy the scenery of Upper Egypt and a short coffee break.

Upon arrival, we begin with a guided visit to the Valley of the Kings, where many pharaohs were buried.

Then we continue to the impressive Temple of Queen Hatshepsut, one of the most remarkable female rulers in history.

Next stop is at the Colossi of Memnon, two massive stone statues that guard the Theban necropolis.

You will then enjoy lunch at a local restaurant with authentic Egyptian dishes.

After lunch, we head to the Karnak Temple complex – the largest religious building ever constructed.

For those who wish, an optional Nile boat ride is available (extra cost).

In the late afternoon, we return to Hurghada and you will arrive back at your hotel in the evening.

Program of Luxor Tour & Hot Air Balloon Ride

Highlights of the Tour

  • Early morning Hot Air Balloon Ride over Luxor
  • Valley of the Kings
  • Temple of Queen Hatshepsut
  • Colossi of Memnon
  • Lunch at a local restaurant
  • Karnak Temple

Description for Luxor Tour & Hot Air Balloon Ride

We pick you up from your hotel in Hurghada very early in the morning and transfer you to Luxor.

Upon arrival, you will experience an unforgettable Hot Air Balloon Ride at sunrise, offering breathtaking panoramic views over the Nile Valley and Luxor’s temples.

After landing, we continue with a guided tour of the Valley of the Kings, where the pharaohs of the New Kingdom were buried.

Next, visit the stunning Temple of Queen Hatshepsut, built into the cliffs of Deir el-Bahari.

A stop will also be made at the Colossi of Memnon for photos and history insights.

You will then enjoy lunch at a local Egyptian restaurant.

In the afternoon, we explore the vast Karnak Temple complex, dedicated to Amun-Ra, the Sun God.

At the end of the day, you will return back to your hotel in Hurghada in the evening.
