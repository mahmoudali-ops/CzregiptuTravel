Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride

This 3-hour tour is an extremely dusty adventure with absolute fun guaranteed!

The 3 Hour Safari by Quad Bike is a great opportunity to see the desert in all its beauty and splendor. The Safari by Quad Bike Tour is for those who want to cross the desert on a four-wheeled ATV.

During the Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride, you can explore the life and traditions of the Bedouin village. And also ride a camel and learn interesting facts about their life in the desert.

Memories and impressions of the excursion Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride will stay with you for a long time!

Program of 3 Hours Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride

Time Program
⏰ 7:30 Pick up from the hotel
⏰ 8:00 Arrival at Safari Station
⏰ 11:00 Departure to the hotel
⏰ 11:30 Back in hotel

Description for 3 Hours Safari by Quad Bike & Camel Ride tour

We will pick you up from your hotel at about 07.30 am and bring you to the Quad Bike training station...

All Quad Bikes have an automatic control system...

We drive about 1 hour (approx. 25 km) with Quad Bike until we arrive at the Bedouin village.

Friendly Bedouins will meet and greet you...

In the same place you will have the opportunity to ride camels...

After that, we will bring you back to your hotel.
