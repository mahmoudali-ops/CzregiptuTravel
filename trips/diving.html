COURSE OVERVIEW

If you’ve always wanted to take scuba diving lessons, experience unparalleled adventure and see the world beneath the waves, this is where it starts. Get your scuba diving certification with the PADI Open Water Diver course – the world’s most popular and widely recognized scuba course.

Millions of people have learned to scuba dive and gone on to discover the wonders of the aquatic world through this course. The PADI Open Water Diver course consists of three main phases: the Knowledge Development, Confined Water Dives to learn basic scuba skills and Open Water Dives to use your skills and explore.

What's Included

  • PADI course materials and certification
  • Transfer from/to any hotel in Hurghada
  • Full diving equipment

What's Excluded

  • Transfer from/to any hotel in El Gouna, Sahl Hasheesh, Makadi Bay, and Soma Bay 10€ (per person/per day)

Learning Materials

There are two options to choose your own material in various languages:

  • Book: The PADI Open Water Diver Manual (Book, RDP and RDP Manual) and 5 videos (To be watched through our Dive Center). Both are to be finished upon course completion.
  • eLearning: The PADI eLearning gives you the ability to start the course online before starting your vacation. Start reading and watching the video before you can start diving. You can access your PADI eLearning through the PADI App or on web browsers on any device. Our PADI Instructor will schedule time with you to check your progress and make sure you understand important scuba diving information.

Speed Boat Specifications

The speed boat is a 9 meters RIB (Rigid Inflatable Boat) designed specially for diving, with a maximum capacity of 8 divers. It is equipped with two Yamaha 115 HP engines, allowing us to access dive sites in Hurghada within minutes. The boat is built to maintain a stable ride even through the roughest of waters, with a lightweight design that adapts and maneuvers through waves comfortably.

Half Day Speed Boat Trips durations: 1 dive trip takes 2 Hours - 2 dives takes 4 Hours - 3 dives trip takes 6 Hours

Emergency Ready

  • Communication device
  • GPS
  • Life Jackets
  • First Aid kit
  • 100% Oxygen

Why Dive with a Speed Boat?

  • Top dive sites are a priority
  • Fast trip (time saver)
  • Always plan to dive with the current (drift)
