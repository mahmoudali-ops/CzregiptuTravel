Description for Orange Bay Hurghada Snorkeling Trip

At around 8:30 we will pick you up from your hotel and take you to the pier. On a luxuriously equipped boat we sail to the Orange Bay Island.

During the trip, you will snorkel on beautiful coral reefs. Next, we will take you to Orange Bay Island by small boat.

You will spend about 2 hours on Orange Bay Island. Relax on the beautiful sandy beach or go snorkeling around the island.

We then leave the island and take a boat trip to enjoy lunch, then continue by boat for a second snorkeling trip.

At about 16:00 we return to the pier and the driver will be waiting for us there, who will take us to the hotel.

Mahmya Island Hurghada Snorkeling Trip

Snorkeling at Mahmya Island

Lunch

Description for Mahmya Island Hurghada Snorkeling Trip

In the morning we will pick you up from your hotel and take you to the pier. On board, you will be greeted by a friendly crew who will provide you with snorkel equipment (mask and fins) for an exciting snorkeling adventure. Dive into the pristine underwater world and enjoy a variety of snorkeling spots!

Set sail for the Red Sea, where the captain plots a course for Mahmya Island. Upon arrival, disembark to explore the picturesque island and unique natural landscape. Then enjoy a hearty buffet lunch at the waterfront restaurant.

After lunch on Mahmya Island, you will have the opportunity to snorkel in the azure waters or feel the fine sand between your toes as you stroll along the beach. Enjoy the sun and the idyllic sandy beach. Enjoy a cool drink and swim in the Red Sea from time to time. Admire the scenic natural beauty, azure sea and idyllic island of this uninhabited island.

After an amazing tour of Mahmya Island Hurghada, you will return to the port in the late afternoon, where you will be transferred back to your hotel.

Fishing trip by boat – go fishing in the Red Sea off Hurghada with three stops

Experience

Experience an exciting boat trip from Hurghada. You have the opportunity to fish in the open sea at three different locations in the Red Sea.

After breakfast we pick you up in the hotel with our comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle and take you directly to the fishing boat for your great excursion.

Fishing

You have three stops to fish in the open sea. In case you’re not in the mood to fish, you can simply sunbathe on the deck or go snorkeling. If you catch an eatable fish, our cook will prepare it for you right on the boat!

Types of Fish

  • Tuna
  • Sailfish
  • Grouper

Fishing Trip in Hurghada

The Red Sea, however, has a lot more in store. For the professional angler, Hurghada is one of the dream fishing destinations.

The numerous, huge coral reefs, which are located not far from the coast, are home to many large fish species. In addition to the tuna already mentioned, these also include perches, bonitos and further in the south of the Red Sea even sharks. An angler gets his money’s worth here.

You don’t have much fishing experience yet? No problem. Of course, our experienced Egyptian fishermen will be happy to help and advise you on board. In case you are not only interested in fishing, but just enjoy the variety of fish and love the sea, this is the place for you.

Accompanying Persons

Accompanying persons who do not want to fish themselves can sunbathe on the deck of the ship or go snorkeling, of course; the nearby reefs are ideal for this. If you can catch an eatable fish, the experienced cooks on board will be happy to prepare the fish directly.

  • Fishing trip by boat with three stops
  • Snorkeling equipment
  • Guide assistance
  • Lunch aboard the boat
  • Mineral water and soft drinks

What is Not Included

  • Tips for guides & Transfer from Makadi bay, Elgouna 10 Euro per Person
  • Personal items, meals, drinks or services not mentioned
